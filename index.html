The Top Four Restuarants in the DMV

These are my top four picks for restuarants in the DC-Maryland-Virginia area. UMBC's campus is displayed on the map as a reference point for other students.


Number 1: Mandalay Restuarant in Silver Spring. It is family owned and serves Burmese cuisine. Sadly, it will be closing in the coming months so visit QUICK!! The menu can be found here: https://www.mandalayrestaurantcafe.com/menu

Number 2: Jyoti Indian Cuisine in Adams Morgan DC. As the name implies they serve Indian cuisine. You wouldn't notice it if you weren't looking, but it is a gem of the city and for vegetarians. The menu can be found here: https://www.jyotidc.com/menu/

Number 3: Moby Dick Sushi. A wonderful, yet small establishment hidden in Wheaton. But if you are looking for good sushi, it is the place to go! The menu can be found here: https://www.mbdsushi.com/

Number 4: Farmers & Distillers located just outside Chinatown DC. All and all wonderful spot for dinner or brunch. A little pricier, but for sure worth it. The menu can be found here: https://farmersanddistillers.com/menu/
